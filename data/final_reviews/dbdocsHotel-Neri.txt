Hotel name: Hotel Neri 

Hotel name:  Hotel Neri is a charming boutique hotel located in the heart of Barcelona's Gothic Quarter. Guests rave about its excellent location, with many attractions and restaurants within walking distance. The hotel's beautiful decor, which blends modern and historic elements, is also highly praised. The staff is consistently described as friendly, helpful, and accommodating, going above and beyond to make guests feel welcome and comfortable. The rooms are generally well-appointed and comfortable, although some guests found them to be on the small side. The rooftop terrace and the restaurant are also popular amenities. While some guests found the breakfast to be overpriced, most agreed that the overall experience at Hotel Neri was excellent. 

Hotel Address: San Sever 5 Ciutat Vella 08002 Barcelona Spain
