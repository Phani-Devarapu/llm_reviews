Hotel name: Aparthotel Arai 4 Superior 

Hotel name:  Hotel Arai in Barcelona offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern amenities. Guests rave about the stunning design, spacious rooms, and well-equipped kitchenettes. The location in the heart of the Gothic Quarter is ideal for exploring the city, with many restaurants, bars, and attractions within walking distance. The staff is consistently praised for their exceptional service and willingness to go above and beyond to assist guests. The rooftop terrace and pool provide a relaxing retreat from the bustling city below. While some guests found the rooms to be a bit small or noisy due to the lively neighborhood, the overall experience at Hotel Arai is highly recommended. 

Hotel Address: Avinyo 30 Ciutat Vella 08002 Barcelona Spain
