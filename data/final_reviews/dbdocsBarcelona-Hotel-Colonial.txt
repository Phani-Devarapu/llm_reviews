Hotel name: Barcelona Hotel Colonial 

Hotel name:  Overall, the hotel received mixed reviews from guests. While its location was consistently praised for being close to the beach, Gothic Quarter, and various attractions, some guests found the rooms to be small, dimly lit, and lacking basic amenities like kettles and complimentary water. The hotel's breakfast also received mixed feedback, with some guests finding it to be poor in terms of choice and value for money. The hotel staff, however, was generally described as friendly and helpful. Guests also appreciated the cleanliness and comfort of the rooms, as well as the hotel's convenient location. However, some guests mentioned issues with noise, especially in rooms facing the street or located near the train tracks. Additionally, the hotel's private parking was noted to be quite a distance away from the hotel itself.
