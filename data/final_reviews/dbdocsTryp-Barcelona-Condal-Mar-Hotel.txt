Hotel name: Tryp Barcelona Condal Mar Hotel 

Hotel name:  The hotel received mixed reviews from guests. Positive comments highlighted the comfortable beds, spacious rooms, cleanliness, friendly and helpful staff, extensive breakfast options, rooftop pool, and proximity to the beach and shopping center. Negative feedback mentioned issues with air conditioning, mosquitoes, poor mini-bar quality, lack of nearby restaurants and metro stations, and a few instances of poor service. Some guests also found the location to be inconvenient and the surrounding area to be rough. Overall, the hotel offers a good value for money, but its location may not be ideal for those seeking a more central or lively area.
