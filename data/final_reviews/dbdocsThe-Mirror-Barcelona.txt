Hotel name: The Mirror Barcelona 

Hotel name:  The Mirror Barcelona hotel offers a unique and modern experience for its guests. The hotel's striking feature is the extensive use of mirrors throughout the design, creating a visually captivating atmosphere. Guests have praised the comfortable beds, cleanliness, and friendly staff. The rooftop pool, sauna, and solarium provide relaxation options, while the location near restaurants, cafes, and the metro station offers convenience. However, some guests have mentioned the lack of tea and coffee-making facilities in the rooms, and the small size of the rooftop pool. Overall, The Mirror Barcelona seems to offer a stylish and enjoyable stay for those seeking a contemporary and centrally located hotel experience. 

Hotel Address: Corcega 255 Eixample 08036 Barcelona Spain
