Hotel name: NH Hesperia Barcelona del Mar 

Hotel name:  The hotel is situated in a very convenient location, just a few minutes walk from the beach and close to public transportation. The staff is friendly and helpful, and the breakfast buffet is delicious with a wide variety of options. The rooms are clean and comfortable, with some offering stunning sea views. However, some guests have complained about the small size of the rooms, the lack of tea and coffee making facilities, and the expensive parking. Additionally, the hotel does not have a pool or fitness center, which may be a drawback for some travelers. Overall, the hotel offers good value for money and is a great option for those looking for a comfortable stay near the beach. 

Hotel Address: Espronceda 6 Sant Mart 08005 Barcelona Spain
