Hotel name: Melia Barcelona Sky 4 Sup 

Hotel name:  The Melia Sky Hotel in Barcelona offers a modern and comfortable stay with stunning city views. Guests praise the friendly and helpful staff, the rooftop pool, and the comfortable beds. The hotel is located a bit further from the city center, but it's close to the metro and taxis are readily available. Some guests found the breakfast to be expensive and the rooms to be a bit basic, but overall, the hotel provides a great experience for those looking for a stylish and comfortable stay in Barcelona. 

Hotel Address: Diagonal Pere IV 272 Sant Mart 08005 Barcelona Spain
