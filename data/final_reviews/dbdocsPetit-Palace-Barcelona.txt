Hotel name:  The hotel received mixed reviews from guests. Many praised its location, with several reviewers noting that it was close to major attractions, restaurants, and shopping. The staff was also frequently commended for their helpfulness and friendliness. The hotel's breakfast was also a highlight for many guests, with several reviewers praising the variety and quality of the food. However, some guests found the rooms to be small and in need of updating, and there were complaints about noise from the street and from other guests. Additionally, some guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the amenities offered. Overall, the hotel seems to offer a convenient and comfortable stay in a great location, but some guests may be disappointed by the size and condition of the rooms.
