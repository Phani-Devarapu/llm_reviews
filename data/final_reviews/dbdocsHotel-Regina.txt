Hotel name: Hotel Regina 

Hotel name:  Hotel Regina, located in the heart of Barcelona, offers guests a comfortable and convenient stay. While some reviewers found the rooms to be a bit small, they were generally well-equipped and clean. The hotel's location is praised for being close to many attractions, shopping areas, and public transportation. Guests also appreciated the friendly and helpful staff, who were always willing to assist with any requests or provide recommendations. The breakfast buffet was considered to be good, with a variety of options to choose from. However, some guests mentioned that the soundproofing in the rooms could be improved, as noise from the hallway or neighboring rooms could be disruptive. Overall, Hotel Regina offers a pleasant stay in a convenient location, making it a solid choice for both leisure and business travelers. 

Hotel Address: Bergara 2 4 Eixample 08002 Barcelona Spain
