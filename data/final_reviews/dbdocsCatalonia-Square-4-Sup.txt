Hotel name: Catalonia Square 4 Sup 

Hotel name:  Overall, Hotel Catalonia Square received positive reviews from guests who stayed there. The hotel is praised for its excellent location, with many attractions and transportation options within walking distance. The staff is consistently described as friendly, helpful, and accommodating, going above and beyond to make guests feel welcome and comfortable. The rooms are generally described as clean, spacious, and well-equipped, with comfortable beds and modern amenities. Many guests also appreciated the free afternoon snacks and buffet, as well as the small rooftop pool and terrace area. A few minor complaints were mentioned, such as the lack of soundproofing between rooms and the small size of the pool, but these did not significantly detract from the overall positive experience of most guests. 

Hotel Address: Ronda Sant Pere 9 Eixample 08010 Barcelona Spain
