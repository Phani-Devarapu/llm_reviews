Hotel name: Alma Barcelona GL 

Hotel name:  The hotel received mixed reviews from guests. Many praised the comfortable beds, spacious rooms, and free soft drinks in the minibar. The location was also seen as a positive, with the hotel being close to many tourist attractions and shopping areas. However, some guests found the breakfast to be overpriced and the hotel's modern design to be a bit too dark. There were also complaints about the lack of privacy in the bathrooms, as some rooms did not have doors separating the toilet and shower from the rest of the room. Additionally, some guests experienced issues with the fingerprint door opening system. Overall, the hotel seems to offer a comfortable and stylish stay, but there are some areas where improvements could be made to enhance the guest experience.
