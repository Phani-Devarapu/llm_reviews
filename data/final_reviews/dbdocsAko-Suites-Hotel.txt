Hotel name: Ako Suites Hotel 

Hotel name:  Ako Suites, a modern aparthotel in Barcelona, offers spacious and comfortable suites with separate living areas and kitchenettes. Guests praise the hotel's excellent location near Plaza Catalunya, Las Ramblas, and various restaurants and shops. The friendly and helpful staff is often mentioned as a highlight, providing valuable recommendations and assistance throughout the stay. The rooftop pool and terrace provide a relaxing escape, although some guests found it small and crowded. While the rooms are generally clean and well-equipped, a few complaints were raised regarding the lack of privacy due to glass bathroom doors and the occasional noise from the street or neighboring rooms. Overall, Ako Suites offers good value for money and is recommended for those seeking a comfortable and convenient stay in Barcelona. 

Hotel Address: Diputaci 195 Eixample 08011 Barcelona Spain
