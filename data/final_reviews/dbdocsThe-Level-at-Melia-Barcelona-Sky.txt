Hotel name: The Level at Melia Barcelona Sky 

Hotel name:  The hotel offers a range of experiences, with some guests praising the excellent service, stunning views, and luxurious amenities such as the exclusive Level facility and spa. Others, however, criticize the high prices, lack of value for money, and inconvenient location away from city attractions. The breakfast receives mixed reviews, with some finding it amazing while others complain about its quality and value. The rooms are generally comfortable, but some guests mention issues with temperature control and dampness. The staff is often praised for their friendliness and helpfulness, but there are occasional complaints about their efficiency and the accuracy of information provided. Overall, the experiences at this hotel vary greatly, with some guests highly satisfied and others disappointed.
