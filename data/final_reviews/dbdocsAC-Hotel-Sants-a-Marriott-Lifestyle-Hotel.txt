Hotel name: AC Hotel Sants a Marriott Lifestyle Hotel 

Hotel name:  The hotel is situated right next to the Sants station, making it convenient for travelers. The rooms are modern, clean, and well-appointed, offering a comfortable stay. Guests have praised the excellent breakfast options and the helpfulness of the staff. However, some have noted that the front desk staff could be friendlier and more responsive to communication. Additionally, there have been complaints about the lack of tea and coffee-making facilities in the rooms, as well as the absence of a restaurant or eatery within the hotel. While the location is convenient for train travel, some guests have expressed concerns about the surrounding area, describing it as dodgy or noisy. Overall, the hotel provides a comfortable and convenient stay for those prioritizing proximity to the train station, but there are some areas where improvements could be made to enhance the guest experience. 

Hotel Address: Passeig de Sant Antoni 36 40 Sants Montju c 08014 Barcelona Spain
