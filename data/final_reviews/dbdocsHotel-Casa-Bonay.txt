Hotel name: Hotel Casa Bonay 

Hotel name:  Casa Bonay, a trendy boutique hotel in Barcelona, offers a unique and stylish stay for its guests. The hotel's interior design blends Scandinavian and Mediterranean influences, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Guests can enjoy comfortable beds, spacious rooms, and modern amenities such as free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. The hotel also features a rooftop terrace, a bar, and a restaurant serving delicious local cuisine. While some guests found the rooms to be a bit small and lacking in storage space, the overall experience at Casa Bonay is highly rated, with praise for the friendly and helpful staff, the excellent breakfast, and the convenient location near public transportation and popular tourist attractions. 

Hotel Address: Gran Via de les Corts Catalanes 700 Eixample 08010 Barcelona Spain
