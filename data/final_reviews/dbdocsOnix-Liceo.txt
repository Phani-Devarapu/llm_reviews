Hotel name: Onix Liceo 

Hotel name:  The hotel is situated in a very convenient location, just off La Ramblas, making it an ideal base for exploring Barcelona. The staff is generally friendly and helpful, and the hotel provides basic amenities such as a swimming pool and a complimentary breakfast. However, some guests have expressed concerns about the noise levels, particularly from the street and from other guests, as well as the lack of basic amenities in the rooms such as tea and coffee making facilities. Additionally, some guests have reported issues with the cleanliness of the rooms and the quality of the breakfast. Overall, while the hotel offers a good location and friendly staff, there are some areas where improvements could be made to enhance the guest experience.
