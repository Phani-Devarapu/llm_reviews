Hotel name: Hotel Vilamar  

Hotel name:  The Hotel Vilamari is situated in a prime location near the Plaza Espanya, offering easy access to the city's attractions and transportation. Guests praise the hotel's cleanliness, friendly and helpful staff, and the delicious breakfast spread. However, some rooms, particularly those facing the atrium or with windows facing the quad, can be noisy and lack privacy due to glass doors and thin walls. Additionally, some guests have experienced issues with the air conditioning, lack of safes in the rooms, and inconsistent housekeeping services. Despite these drawbacks, the hotel's convenient location and comfortable rooms make it a generally positive choice for travelers seeking a comfortable stay in Barcelona. 

Hotel Address: Vilamari 34 36 Eixample 08015 Barcelona Spain
