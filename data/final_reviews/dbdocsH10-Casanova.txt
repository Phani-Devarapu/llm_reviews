Hotel name: H10 Casanova 

Hotel name:  The H10 Casanova hotel in Barcelona offers a range of experiences for its guests. Positive reviews mention the comfortable beds, spacious rooms, and friendly staff. The rooftop pool and bar area are popular amenities, and the location is praised for its convenience to public transportation and attractions. The hotel also offers a complimentary glass of cava upon arrival and a varied breakfast buffet. Negative reviews mention issues with room cleanliness, noise, and limited amenities such as tea and coffee-making facilities in the rooms. Some guests also found the pool to be too small and crowded. Overall, the H10 Casanova provides a comfortable and convenient stay for travelers, with some areas for improvement in terms of cleanliness and facilities. 

Hotel Address: Gran Via 559 Eixample 08012 Barcelona Spain
