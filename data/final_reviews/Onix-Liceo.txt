Hotel name: Onix Liceo 

Hotel name:  The hotel has a great location, just off Las Ramblas, and is close to many attractions. The staff is friendly and helpful, and the rooms are clean and comfortable. The breakfast is good, with a variety of options. However, some guests have complained about the noise from the street and from other guests, as well as the lack of air conditioning in some rooms. Additionally, the pool is small and not suitable for swimming. Overall, the hotel offers a good value for the price, but guests should be aware of the potential drawbacks before booking. 

Hotel Address: Nou de la Rambla 36 Ciutat Vella 08001 Barcelona Spain
