Hotel name: Hotel Indigo Barcelona Plaza Catalunya 

Hotel name:  Hotel Indigo Barcelona - Plaza Catalunya receives mixed reviews from travelers. Many guests praise the hotel's excellent location, just a short walk from Las Ramblas and other major attractions. The staff is often described as friendly and helpful, and the rooms are generally clean and comfortable. The hotel also offers a rooftop pool and terrace, which many guests enjoy. However, some guests complain that the rooms are small and dark, and that the breakfast is overpriced. Additionally, some guests have experienced issues with noise from adjacent rooms or the street. Overall, Hotel Indigo Barcelona - Plaza Catalunya offers a convenient and comfortable stay in a great location, but some guests may be disappointed by the small room size and lack of natural light.
