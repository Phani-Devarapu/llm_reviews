Hotel name: Abba Garden 

Hotel name:  The Abba Garden Hotel offers clean and comfortable rooms with stunning views, a great swimming pool, and a delicious breakfast spread. The staff is generally friendly and helpful, making guests feel welcome and taken care of. However, some guests have experienced issues with the air conditioning, lack of tea and coffee facilities in the rooms, and inconsistent Wi-Fi. Additionally, the hotel's location is a bit far from the city center and public transportation, making it less convenient for tourists exploring Barcelona. 

Hotel Address: Santa Rosa Esplugues 33 08950 Barcelona Spain
